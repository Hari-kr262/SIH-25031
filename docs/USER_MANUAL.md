# CivicResolve User Manual

## For Citizens

### Reporting an Issue
1. Open CivicResolve app and login
2. Tap "Report Issue" button
3. Select the issue category (Pothole, Garbage, etc.)
4. Add a photo of the issue
5. Enter the location/address
6. Set priority level
7. Submit — your report is auto-routed to the right department

### Tracking Your Issues
- Go to "My Issues" to see all your reports
- Each issue shows current status and SLA deadline
- You'll receive push notifications on every update

### Voting on Issues
- Browse issues in your area
- Upvote issues that affect you to increase their priority
- Your upvotes earn you 2 points

### Verifying Fixes
- When a fix is uploaded, you'll get notified
- Visit the issue and review the fix photo/video
- Mark as Verified (✅) or Unsatisfactory (❌)
- Give a 1-5 star rating
- Verification earns you 5 points

### Gamification
- Report Issue: +10 pts
- Upvote: +2 pts  
- Verify fix: +5 pts
- Levels: Newcomer → Active Citizen → Community Champion → Civic Leader → City Hero
- Earn badges for milestones!

## For Field Workers

### Viewing Assignments
- Login with your worker credentials
- Dashboard shows all issues assigned to you
- Issues are sorted by priority and deadline

### Updating Issue Status
1. Open assigned issue
2. Tap "Start Work" to change status to "In Progress"
3. Complete the repair
4. Upload photo/video proof (geo-location verified)
5. Submit — citizen will be notified to verify

## For Department Heads

### Managing Team
- View all issues routed to your department
- Assign issues to specific field workers
- Monitor SLA compliance
- Track team performance

### Budget Management
- View allocated budget for your department
- Record expenses against resolved issues
- Generate budget reports
